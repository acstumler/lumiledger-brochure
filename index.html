Target market

Who we serve

U.S. small businesses with roughly 1–25 employees that need clean financials and KPIs without the complexity of full accounting suites.

Typically owner- or ops-led, managing books after hours with limited bookkeeping support.

Cash-basis by default: Most firms this size operate on a cash basis. LumiLedger produces cash-basis statements out of the box.

Why it matters

~6.3M employer small businesses represent 99.9% of U.S. companies. The median firm holds <15 days of cash buffer, making fast, accurate statements critical.

~71% carry debt and many report cash-flow strain — real-time visibility and lender-ready exports reduce friction.
