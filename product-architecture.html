Product architecture

LumiLedger is designed as a complete financial preparation and interpretation platform.

Its architecture reflects that intent. Each component exists to improve clarity, preserve traceability, and support professional review while maintaining transparency and accuracy.

It is reliability through simplicity.

Design principles

LumiLedger's architecture is guided by a small set of principles:

Source-first

All financial activity is anchored to a known source account. Nothing is inferred without context.

Transparency over automation

Every classification and calculation is reviewable. The system never hides uncertainty.

Deterministic where accuracy matters

Traditional accounting logic is used wherever outcomes must be consistent and explainable.

Interpretation without authority

LumiLedger explains results but does not finalize them.

Simplicity at the surface

Complexity is handled in the background so users are not burdened with it.

These principles govern all downstream behavior.

How data flows through LumiLedger

LumiLedger follows a deliberate, sequential flow from raw activity to understanding:

1. Account connection

Users connect the financial accounts used in the business, including bank accounts, credit cards, and loans. These accounts establish the known sources of financial activity.

LumiLedger uses Plaid Secure, API-based data sharing for all financial connections, ensuring security, stability, and efficiency in how transaction data is accessed and maintained.

2. Transaction ingestion

Transaction data is ingested directly from connected sources. Each transaction retains its original attributes and source context.

3. Source anchoring

Every transaction is first anchored to its source account. This establishes one side of the journal entry and prevents loss of traceability.

4. Classification logic

The offsetting side of each entry is determined using structured logic and machine-learned classification. Uncertainty is preserved rather than concealed.

5. Structured outputs

Once entries are established, LumiLedger produces structured financial views that can be reviewed, adjusted, and exported.

No step assumes completeness or correctness without visibility.

Journal logic and classification

At its core, LumiLedger applies standard journal-entry logic.

For each transaction:

  • One side of the entry is assigned to a known source account
  • The opposing side is classified based on transaction context

Classification is treated as a suggestion, not a mandate. Results are transparent, editable, and traceable back to original activity.

LumiLedger favors consistency and explainability over aggressive automation. When certainty is low, that uncertainty is surfaced rather than masked.

Financial statement generation

From structured journal entries, LumiLedger generates core financial statements for analysis and review, including:

  • Income Statement
  • Balance Sheet
  • General Ledger views

These statements are designed to be:

  • Internally consistent
  • Chronologically coherent
  • Exportable for professional use

They are designed for understanding, decision-making, and professional review.

Interpretation and storytelling

Once financial structure is established, LumiLedger applies an interpretation layer.

Key metrics are calculated in the background and translated into a plain-English financial narrative that explains:

  • What changed
  • When it changed
  • Why it matters

This narrative is contextual and time-aware, comparing performance day over day, month over month, quarter over quarter, and year over year based only on available data.

The goal is comprehension, not instruction.

Designed with intentional boundaries

LumiLedger is designed to maintain clarity and transparency.

While LumiLedger provides complete financial preparation and reporting, it preserves the role of professional judgment:

  • Accounting elections and policies remain with the professional
  • Book closing and compliance readiness are confirmed by professionals
  • Final review and sign-off remain with accounting professionals

This approach allows LumiLedger to remain flexible, explainable, and compatible with a wide range of professional workflows.
